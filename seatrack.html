Seatrack

Project Overview


The Product:

SeaTrack is one of my first personal projects. It is a user experience focused on helping small businesses combat rising sea levels. SeaTrack provides businesses with resources, allows them to contribute to causes, track business analytics, and learn about rising sea levels.

The Problem:

Rising sea levels affect small businesses by decreasing the amount of in person customers they get due to flooding.

My Role:

UX Designer in this personal project leading the app and responsive website from conception to delivery.

Duration:

Personal Project August 2022 - September 2022

The Goal:

SeaTrack aims to provide businesses with resources, learnings, causes, and analytical tracking to help them thrive against rising sea levels.

Responsibilities:

Conducting interviews, paper and digital wire-framing, low and high fidelity prototyping, conducting usability studies, accounting for accessibility, iterating on designs, determining information architecture, and responsive design.
